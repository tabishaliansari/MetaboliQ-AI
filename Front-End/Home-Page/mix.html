Overview

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs when the pancreas does not produce enough insulin or when the body cannot effectively use the insulin it produces. MetabolicQ AI helps track and predict your health by assessing key metrics and providing personalized insights.

What MetaboliQ-AI Does

Analyzes Key Health Metrics:

Metabolic AI evaluates essential inputs such as age, body mass index (BMI), blood sugar levels, family history, and lifestyle habits to assess diabetes risk.

Generates Personalized Insights:

The platform suggests actionable next steps, such as lifestyle changes or consulting healthcare professionals, tailored to your unique health profile..
